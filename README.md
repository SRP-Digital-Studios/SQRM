# SQRM - Saksham QR Menu

Smart Restaurant QR Menu & Ordering Platform

SQRM (Saksham QR Menu) is a modern QR-based restaurant menu and ordering platform that enables restaurants to create digital menus, receive customer orders, manage restaurants, track analytics, and generate QR codes without requiring customers to create accounts.

Developed and maintained by SRP Digital Studios.
